Privacy Policy – Geography Zone

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Geography Zone is a free geography quiz app. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Geography Zone does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app runs entirely on your device. No account is required, and no data is sent to any server.

Local Storage

The app saves your high scores and preferences locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

Geography Zone is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
